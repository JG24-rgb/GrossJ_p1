About MGBD: Mark Grey's Story

After spending many years in the overnight distribution, transport, and retail industries, Mark Grey founded MGBD Parts & Services in late 2010, driven by his passion for Rover P6 cars produced between 1963 and 1977. By the end of 2010, he was repairing, servicing, and restoring P6s for others while also holding an active volunteer committee position in a classic car club since 1994. In August 2012, we launched our webstore, initially offering just 26 new Rover P6 parts. Today, in 2024, we stock over 2500 new products and have an extensive selection of refurbished and second-hand parts, all aimed at keeping the Rover P6 alive and on the road. We are continuously investing in the remanufacturing of obsolete parts and are gradually expanding our range to include other Rover models.

A Timeless Classic: The Rover P6 Story

The Rover P6 Series I, unveiled in 1963, represented a pioneering venture into executive car design by the British automaker Rover. Serving as the precursor to subsequent iterations, the Series I laid the foundation for the brand's foray into the luxury car market, boasting a blend of innovative engineering and elegant styling. At the heart of the Series I Rover P6 was a commitment to advanced engineering and cutting-edge technology. The car featured a revolutionary suspension system, known as "Rover Suspension", which utilized a De Dion tube at the rear and a wishbone arrangement at the front, resulting in exceptional handling and ride comfort unparalleled in its class at the time. In terms of powertrain options, the Series I P6 offered a range of engine choices to suit diverse driving preferences. From the efficient 2.0-liter inline-four engine to the more spirited 2.2-liter inline-six powerplant, the Series I provided drivers with a selection of engines capable of delivering both performance and fuel economy. Externally, the Series I Rover P6 exuded a timeless elegance, characterized by its sleek lines and understated design cues. The car's iconic shape, featuring a distinctive front grille and classic Rover badge, made it instantly recognizable on the road, further enhancing its appeal to discerning motorists. Inside the cabin, the Series I P6 offered a refined and luxurious environment, with comfortable seating, high-quality materials, and a range of optional extras to cater to individual tastes. From wood veneer trim to plush carpeting, the interior of the Series I exuded a sense of opulence and sophistication befitting its executive status. Overall, the Rover P6 Series I left an indelible mark on the automotive landscape, setting new standards for performance, comfort, and style in the executive car segment. Its innovative design, advanced engineering, and timeless appeal continue to captivate enthusiasts and collectors, cementing its legacy as a classic icon of British motoring history.
